How to guide:
Click the get suggestions button.
If there are suggestion:
Click the "Start" button next to a track. If there are no suggestions:
You are too far from any current tracks, but you might try these:
- Enter "1" to the "Question ID" field,
- click the "Set default" button,
- click the "Get question" button.
A question will appear.
Type your answer, and click the "Submit" button.
Since you are too far, your distance will appear in the response area. (If you happen to have walked close in the meantime, you will be redirected to the next task)


FAQ:
Nothing happens when I click the submit button.
Your location services are not enabled. In Chrome, geolocation is not enabled on non-secure (http) connections.
My distance is NAN.
Please report your system details to the creator.
